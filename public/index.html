HOW YOU SCORE

You earn points when your ORIGINAL four teams win.

Base Points Per Round:

  • Round of 64 = 1 point
  • Round of 32 = 2 points
  • Sweet 16 = 4 points
  • Elite 8 = 7 points
  • Final Four = 12 points
  • Championship = 20 points

Upset Bonus:

If your team beats a higher seed, you earn bonus points equal to: Opponent seed minus your seed (maximum 10 bonus points).

Example: A 12 seed beats a 5 seed = 7 bonus points.

WEEKLY WINNERS

  • Week 1: Round of 64 + Round of 32 ($50 / $25 / $10)
  • Week 2: Sweet 16 + Elite 8 ($50 / $25 / $10)
  • Week 3: Final Four + Championship ($50 / $25 / $10)

Each week has its own winner based on points scored during that week.

WEEK 2 BONUS TEAM

At the start of Week 2, anyone who has lost at least one original team will receive one additional Bonus Team from the remaining field.

  • The Bonus Team counts toward Week 2 and 3 scoring only
  • Does NOT count toward your overall total

This keeps everyone competitive heading into the second weekend.

OVERALL WINNER

The Overall Champion is determined by total points from your ORIGINAL four teams only.

The Week 2 Bonus Team does not affect the overall standings.

Overall Winner Prize: $100
